About

Program

West-Mec is one of the only schools that offers coding to high school students because of this oppertunity it offer students a wide variety of learning experiences. The course starts by teaching lessons of basic computer compnents, and then dives in depth into html, css, and javascript integration, but that is only the beginning.

Business Anchors

West-Mec Coding is a unique campus because we have business anchors. Business anchors are real business working coders that come in and work on campus, but also come in and teach students real world criteria in coding/programming.

Campus

The West-Mec Start campus is designed to have the most up to date technology from Apple laptops to the high tech media productions lab, this campus offers any technological needs.

Cost?

West-Mec is a highly advanced school that teaches concepts, not even covered in most high school computer science courses. This course goes above and beyond even college courses and is asffordabel. In order to enroll, it only cost $60 dollars each year.

Open Door Policy

West-Mec Start has a unique in class system called the open door policy. This policy is designed to give more interaction from the students to visitors. In the middle of class if anyone walks a student greets the said person and shows them what is happening in the classroom. This interaction provides a more common touch and interaction to promote a business environment.
